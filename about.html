About the Flipside

The Flipside News is a collection of several satirical newspapers at different high school, universities, and online. We started in 2005 at Deerfield High School in Deerfield, Illinois with the DHS Flipside. We expanded to Stanford University and Northwestern University in 2008.

We have a distinct satirical and publication format. Our style is inspired by the Onion, but we also include our own creative weekly features and a puzzles section. If you are interested in contacting the Flipside News, or starting a Flipside newspaper at your own high school or college email Jeremy Keeshin.
